The 3P Framework

Every investment is underwritten against three structural filters: Payer (can the product be reimbursed or contracted?), Provider (does a health system want this?), Policy (does regulatory momentum create or protect the market?). Companies that satisfy all three compress time-to-revenue and compress acquisition risk — which is why Suncoast's top performers show outsized IRR despite small check sizes.
