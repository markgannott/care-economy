The Research

Paper I
Repricing the Caregiver Economy
Quantifies the $1.6T opportunity cost of 53.0B unpaid hours. Introduces DAC methodology and Stromberg multipliers to produce first published caregiver productivity estimate using WEP decomposition.

The Hidden Multiplier: Coordination Burden in Unpaid Care

Paper III
Systemic Inefficiency and Caregiver Infrastructure
Maps the federal funding landscape ($244.8M NFCSP against $1.6T burden), the GUIDE model as the first reimbursement bridge, and the structural case for care coordination infrastructure.
