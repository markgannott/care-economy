About

Next-generation platform for ultra-rare genetic diseases.

AlphaRose integrates proprietary gene therapy (MOE + abcDNA™), AI-driven target discovery via RINAE.AI, and an in-house patient-driven R&D flywheel to develop and commercialize precision therapies faster, at a fraction of traditional pharma cost.

Lead program Rosiphersen targets HNRNPH2-RD, a devastating neurodevelopmental disorder causing intellectual disability, seizures, and absent speech. The candidate demonstrates 100% survival rescue in murine models and NCATS-independently-validated efficacy in human iPSC-derived neurons. Currently IND-enabling with Phase 1 targeted by end of 2026.

RareLabs generates $2M+ annually across 16+ fee-for-service customers, funding discovery while building proprietary patient data.
