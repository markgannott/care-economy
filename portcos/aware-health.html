About

Aware Health delivers virtual-first musculoskeletal care targeting the single largest driver of employer healthcare cost. MSK conditions — back pain, joint pain, sports injuries — account for roughly one in three employer healthcare dollars spent. Aware's platform integrates digital physical therapy, care navigation, and outcomes tracking to deliver measurable cost reduction and faster recovery for employed populations.
