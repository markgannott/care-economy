About

The Gleamery is a premium dental hygiene and teeth whitening retail concept operating across multiple locations. The company targets the large segment of consumers who want professional-grade oral health services outside the traditional dental office — at more accessible price points, with a modern retail experience. The model benefits from high repeat visit rates, low clinical complexity, and strong consumer brand economics.
